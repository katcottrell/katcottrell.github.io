Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of Nightlinks?

Nightlinks serves with the mission to help all NYC-area creatures of the night promote themselves, connect with collaborators, and find services within the community.

You can use Nightlinks to:

  • Promote your own work, goods, or services.
  • Connect with others in your line of work for advice, collaboration, and references.
  • Find providers for your goods and service needs.

How is Nightlinks different from mainstream professional networking platforms?

Members of our community have unique advertising needs.
You may not your their services associated with your public profile.
Your experience and qualifications may be unusual.
Your goods and services may be targeted at very specific niches within the community.
You may prefer to collaborate with local, more personal connections.
Nightlinks strives to serve these needs.

Who can be listed on Nightlinks?

Nightlinks welcomes all NYC-area creatures of the night:

  • Citizens of New York City and the greater surrounding American northeast area (New York state, Connecticut, New Jersey, etc). Non-northeasterners are welcome to submit listings if you think they will be of interest to members of the northeast community.
  • Members of all dark and alternative scenes (goths, rivetheads, deathrockers, metalheads, punks, and more)
  • All Nightkind (Magickal and Energy workers, Mystics, Vampyres, Therians, Fae, and more).

Don't worry if you fit in enough. If being listed here would serve you or the community, your presence is welcomed.

Do I have to be an experienced professional to be listed?

We welcome entrepreneurs of all experience levels (although we ask that you represent your services honestly if you are very new).
If you are not currently a paid professional, but are still very experienced or knowledgable about a topic and would like to be listed to support others in the area, Nightlinks gratefully welcomes your listing.

Does Nightlinks screen the people or services listed?

Because anyone can list their work, Nightlinks cannot vet or endorse the character or quality of any of our listings.
You are highly encouraged to do your own research and use your best judgement to find collaborators whose work and values align with your expectations.

What is the Discord? Can I join?

Discord is a social platform where Nightlink members can communicate in private channels just for others in their line of work.
